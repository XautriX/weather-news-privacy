Privacy Policy for Weather News

Last updated: October 28, 2025

Weather News ("we", "our", or "us") operates as a weather forecast service that provides users with weather updates and related information through Facebook Messenger. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Weather News via Facebook Messenger, we may receive basic information that Facebook provides based on your Messenger interaction. This may include:

  • Your public Facebook profile information (such as name and profile picture)
  • Your messages sent to our bot (to respond to your requests)
  • Any location data you voluntarily share to receive local weather forecasts

We do not request or collect additional personal data beyond what is necessary to provide the weather forecast service.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information we receive to:

  • Deliver accurate weather forecasts and updates
  • Improve the performance and reliability of our service
  • Respond to your messages and provide support

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to anyone.

3. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with any legal obligations. Messages and interactions may be stored temporarily to ensure proper functionality of the chatbot.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties. However, your use of Facebook Messenger means that Facebook’s own privacy policy applies to your data use on their platform.

Read Facebook’s policy here: https://www.facebook.com/policy

5. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, please be aware that no online service can guarantee complete security.

6. Your Rights

If you wish to stop using the service, you can simply stop interacting with the Messenger bot or delete the conversation. If you have any privacy concerns, you may contact us through Facebook Messenger.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new “Last updated” date above.
